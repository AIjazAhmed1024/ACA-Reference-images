FAQ's

How does S2S work?
S2S is a mobile app that allows users to rent tools, equipment, and more from fellow users in their area. Users can browse listings, make reservations, and complete transactions securely within the app.
How do I rent an item?
To rent an item, simply browse through the available listings on the app, select the item you need, choose your rental dates, and complete the reservation process. You can then arrange for pickup or delivery with the Product Owner.
How do I list my items for rent?
Listing your items on S2S is easy! Just create an account, upload photos and details of your items, and set your rental price and availability S2S will review and publish. Your items will then be visible to potential Users.
Is it safe to rent and lend items through S2S?
Yes, S2S prioritizes the safety and security of all users. We verify user profiles, provide secure payment processing, and offer user reviews to ensure a trusted rental experience.
What types of items can I rent on S2S?
S2S offers a wide range of items for rent, including tools, equipment, outdoor gear, and more. You'll find everything you need for your DIY projects, outdoor adventures, and everyday needs.
How are rental rates determined?
Rental rates are set by the Product Owners of the items. They can choose their preferred rental price based on the item's value, condition, and demand. Product Owners can compare prices and choose the best option for their needs.
What if I need to cancel a reservation order?
If you need to cancel a reservation order, please refer to the cancellation policy set by the Product Owner of the item. Some reservations may be subject to cancellation fees, while others may allow for free cancellations within a certain timeframe.
What is the maximum duration to cancel the reservation?
The S2S system will allow Owner of the product can cancel within 24hrs from the order placing.
Can I extend the rental period?
Yes, you can extend your rental period if the Product Owner of the item agrees. Simply reach out to them through the app to discuss the extension and any additional fees.
How do I communicate with the Product Owner of an item?
You can communicate with the Product Owner of an item through the app. This allows for easy coordination of pickup, drop-off, and any other details related to the rental.
What if there's an issue with the rented item?
If you encounter any issues with a rented item, please contact the Product Owner immediately to discuss a resolution. If needed, S2S customer support is available to assist and facilitate a fair resolution.
Are there any fees associated with using S2S?
S2S is free to download and use for browsing and listings. However, there may be processing fees and tax fees associated with renting items, which will be clearly displayed before you are placing an order.
How are payments processed on S2S?
Payments on S2S are processed securely through Stripe, with transparent pricing displayed during transactions.
Can I request specific pickup / return times?
Yes, you can contact the Product Owner to arrange specific pickup / return times that work for both parties.
What if I accidentally damage an item during the rental period?
Accidents can happen! If you accidentally damage an item during the rental period, please contact the Product Owner immediately to discuss the issue. Some Product Owners may charge damage costs as per S2S Terms of Service , which can be used to cover damages if necessary.
Can I leave a review for the Product Owner?
Yes, after completing a rental transaction Users can leave reviews and ratings. These reviews help build trust within the community and provide valuable feedback for future transactions.
What if I can't find the item I'm looking for?
If you can't find a specific item you're looking for, try expanding your search radius.
Are there any restrictions on who can rent or list items?
S2S welcomes users of all backgrounds, but there may be age restrictions or other requirements for certain items. Product Owners have the right to set specific rental criteria, such as age limits or rental qualifications.
Can I rent an item for multiple days or weeks?
Yes, you can rent items for extended periods, including multiple days, weeks, or even months. Simply select your desired rental dates when placing an order.
How can I contact S2S customer support?
For any additional questions or assistance, please reach out to customer support at team-s2s@sticks2stones.us. Our team is here to help with any inquiries or concerns you may have.
Can I track the status of my order?
S2S offers a user-friendly order tracking feature, enabling users to monitor their orders in real time. Just Sign into your account, navigate to my orders section, and track the progress of your order.
Can I modify or cancel the order?
Yes, you can navigate to the "My Order" section to access and edit your order details as needed.
